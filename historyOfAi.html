History Of Ai

Information on this pages' source can be found within the citation page.

The first system was made in the year, 1950 by Claude Shannon called; the Theseus.

It was a remote controlled mouse that was able to navigate through a labyinth and remember the course after wards.



Over the years, AI has grown within it's abilities and what it's capable of, image developing being apart of that.



The photo generation begins within 2014, starting off pixely and without color; improving over the course of almost a decade with rapid growth and abilities as it continues to improve.



Over the years, Ai has grown the skills capable of journalism, joke telling and responding to human language.
