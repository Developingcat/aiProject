What do studies show about the future of ai?

Studies show that AI is going to leave an impact almost every job possible. Education is going to be impacted, thus rendering the AI a more transformative example. Healthcare may result with AI being used as a standard tool for those with diagnostic work, other things such as law, transportation, languages-- many things can, will and are being impacted by AI.
